When will the competition take place?

Where will the competition take place?

Can I participate in multiple competitions?
Yes. You are allowed to participate in multiple competition categories as long as you meet the requirements for each one.
Can I use the same robot in multiple competitions?
Yes. The competitions have been designed to support this. Many categories share compatible dimensions and arena specifications, allowing the same robot to compete in multiple events whenever it satisfies the technical requirements.

ABOUT US

The Autonomous Systems Technologies (AST) Club is the first and only scientific club of the National Higher School of Autonomous Systems Technologies. Founded in 2024, it is the student-led hub for innovation in autonomous and intelligent systems. We unite students passionate about pushing the boundaries of what machines can perceive, decide, and execute independently. Our focus is on core technologies enabling autonomy: robotics, embedded systems, real-time control, and artificial intelligence.
